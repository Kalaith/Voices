# Future Improvements - Voice Generator

## Advanced TTS Engine Features
1. **Voice Cloning**: Train custom voices from audio samples with minimal training data
2. **Emotion Control**: Real-time emotion injection (happy, sad, angry, excited) with intensity controls
3. **Multi-Language Support**: Extend to 50+ languages with accent and dialect variations
4. **Voice Aging**: Dynamic age adjustment (child, teen, adult, elderly) for the same base voice
5. **Real-time Voice Conversion**: Live voice transformation during recording or streaming

## Enhanced Audio Quality
6. **Audio Post-Processing Pipeline**: Automatic noise reduction, normalization, and enhancement
7. **Background Music Integration**: Add background music with automatic ducking and mixing
8. **Advanced Audio Effects**: Reverb, echo, modulation, and spatial audio effects
9. **Lossless Audio Export**: Support for FLAC, WAV, and other high-quality formats
10. **Batch Audio Processing**: Apply effects and conversions to multiple files simultaneously

## Voice Library & Management
11. **Cloud Voice Library**: Sync voices across devices with cloud storage integration
12. **Voice Marketplace**: Community-driven voice sharing with rating and discovery features
13. **Voice Versioning**: Track voice parameter changes over time with rollback capabilities
14. **Voice Templates**: Pre-configured voice sets for specific use cases (audiobooks, podcasts, games)
15. **Advanced Search**: Find voices by characteristics (age, gender, accent, emotion)

## Script & Content Features
16. **SSML Support**: Full Speech Synthesis Markup Language support for advanced control
17. **Script Analytics**: Word count, estimated duration, and readability analysis

20. **Multi-Format Import**: Support for DOCX, PDF, and other document formats
